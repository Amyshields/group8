Help With the Website
Logging In

What is my national insurance number?

A few weeks or months before your 16 birthday you will be sent a letter from the government containing your national insurance number.
If you have a national insurance number but can not find it or have a problem with it click here. If you are new to the UK or have a problem regarding your national insurance number click here.

Where do I find my password?

When you register to vote online you should be sent a letter containing your password. If this letter did not arrive or is lost, please contact your local electoral office to be sent another.

How do I use the login page?

Enter your information into the textfields and click the orange login button. If it easier for you to be able to see your password as you type it click the "show password" box to be able to see the password you are typing in. If you make the password visible please ensure you are in a private or secure place and that noone standing behind you can see your information as you input it.
Using the Dashboard

Selecting an election.

Select an election to vote in by clicking an orange button on the dashboard page. If there are multiple elections you are eligible to vote in they will all be listed here, but there may be only one, or none at all. If you can not see an election which you expect to see on your dashboard, double check the dates where voting is open for those elections, if you still believe you cannot see an election which you should be able to, contact your local electoral office.

Logging Out.

To securely log out of the electago system scroll to the bottom of the page and click the "log out" link. This will redirect you to the login page and clear your session. Changes to your vote will be saved.
Filling out the Ballot

How do I save my vote?

Select the radio button beside the candidate or option of your choice and click the button labeled "vote" to cast your vote. The vote will then be saved in our database immediately and you can log out of the electago website by clicking the 'log out' button at the bottom of your screen.

What does spoil vote mean? Why would I spoil my vote?

Select the radio button labelled "spoil vote" and click the "vote" button to submit a spoilt vote. Spoiling your vote means that you will be counted as having voted but not for any candidate. This is usually used as a form of protest. If you accidentally submit a spoilt vote you can return to the ballot page and change the vote.
